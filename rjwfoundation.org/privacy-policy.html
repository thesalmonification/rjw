Privacy Policy

RJW Foundation has policies in place that ensure the utmost security and safety to protect your personal information.


OUR PROMISE TO YOU

We promise that we’ll never sell, exchange, transfer and information you give us to another company without first seeking your consent. We use a variety of methods to ensure that no one other than us has access to the information you’ve provided.

There are times when we use the services of a third party service to repair and update our website. We make it very clear that this third party isn’t permitted to use any information provided by our visitors.


PRIVACY PROTECTION ACT COMPLIANCE


Third Party Links

When you visit our website, there are times when you’ll see a link that leads to a service or product provided by another website or company. While we think these third party links will interest our visitor, we also have a duty to inform you that clicking on these links leads you to another website that has different privacy policies than ourselves.


California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

Because we value your privacy we have taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.


Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act)—we do not collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age. Our website, products and services are all directed to people who are at least 13 years old or older.


Google Analytics

We are currently using Google Analytics to analyze the audience of the website as we continually improve our content.

No personal information is collected from Google Analytics. For further information on the privacy policy concerning Google Analytics, learn more here: https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245?hl=en


How We Gather Your Information

We use a form for you to fill out to gain your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address. We have made it possible for you to use our website without ever providing any information at all.


Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our privacy policy.


COOKIES & DISCLOSURES

We have set up our website that when you visit, small files are placed on your computer’s hard drive via your web browser. The cookies increase our website’s ability to quickly serve your needs by quickly remembering the information you’ve provided.


The cookies allow us to:

  • Track advertisements
  • To identify ways we can improve our website
  • Compile information about traffic our website receives and interactions that took place 


If the idea of having our website install cookies on your website, you can change your settings so you get a warning before our website installs the cookie and you can block it. Blocking the cookie may make it difficult to access some services provided by our website, however calling us and speaking to a live person will provide you another means for gaining the information you’re looking for.
